My First Day at ITM Skills University

Excitement filled the air as I stepped into the gates of ITM Skills University, ready to embark on a new chapter of my academic journey. The morning sun illuminated the campus, signaling the beginning of a promising day.

At 9:30 AM, just before the commencement of classes at 10:00 AM, I eagerly walked into the college, fueled by a passion to learn and grow.

As I entered the classroom, I chose the first bench, determined to make the most of every opportunity. Conversations buzzed around me as I made new friends, exchanging stories and aspirations.

Our professors, Sumit Sir and Sheetal Mam, greeted us warmly, setting a welcoming tone for the day ahead. We began with a session led by our director, Sai Kiran Sir, who introduced us to the university's ethos and the exciting curriculum that awaited us.

Sumit Sir initiated our learning journey with a captivating session on binary operations. Eager to absorb new knowledge, I actively participated, answering questions and engaging with the material.

After an intellectually stimulating morning, we gathered in the canteen for lunch, sharing laughter and stories over delicious meals. The camaraderie among classmates grew stronger with each passing moment.

Resuming our classes after the break, we delved deeper into our subjects, captivated by the wealth of information presented to us.

As the clock struck 6:30 PM, marking the end of our lectures, we bid farewell to each other, promising to meet again and continue our journey together.
